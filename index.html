Game Description

Welcome to Rail Game, an exciting grid-based puzzle game where your goal is to build a functional railway network across a challenging landscape. Connect the tracks, avoid obstacles, and make sure your trains can smoothly navigate without interruption. The game offers multiple difficulty levels, with more complex puzzles as you advance. Test your planning and problem-solving skills as you race against time to complete the track!

Use left click to place tiles on the grid and right click to rotate them, aligning the tracks properly. Ensure all connections are smooth; mismatched tracks will prevent the train from moving forward. Once you complete a functional track, your completion time is recorded and added to the leaderboard. Aim to finish quickly and challenge yourself at higher difficulty levels to secure a top spot!
